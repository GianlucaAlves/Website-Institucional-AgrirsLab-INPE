Mapping Amazonian Biodiversity at multiple scales through the integration of geological and ecological data

Develop and explore remote sensing techniques that are useful for characterizing sedimentary cover areas in Brazilian territory, aiming to reconstruct their formation environments over time and space. The goal is to create a solid database that contributes to the analysis of probable factors that acted in the evolution of physical environments throughout geological time. These factors mainly include climate fluctuations, sea level variations, and tectonic movements on local and regional scales...

Status: In progress
Start date: 31-10-2007
