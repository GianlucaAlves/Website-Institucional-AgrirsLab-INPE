Welcome to AgriRSLab

Agricultural Remote Sensing Laboratory – INPE

SCIENCE & TECHNOLOGY

We develop research focused on producing science and technology based on Remote Sensing and Geoinformatics, aiming to generate basic and applied knowledge for the agricultural sector.

SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT

Our goal is to develop and enhance methodologies that foster the sustainable development of Brazilian agriculture, increasing productivity and competitiveness.

QUALIFIED TRAINING

We contribute to the training of highly qualified Master’s and PhD researchers through the INPE Graduate Program in Remote Sensing (PGSER).
