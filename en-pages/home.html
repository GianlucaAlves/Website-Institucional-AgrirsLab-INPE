Welcome to AgriRSLab

Agricultural Remote Sensing Laboratory - INPE

SCIENCE & TECHNOLOGY

We develop research focused on producing science and technology based on Remote Sensing and Geoinformatics, aiming to generate basic and applied knowledge for the agricultural sector.

SUSTAINABLE ADVANCEMENT

Our goal is to develop and improve methodologies that promote the sustainable advancement of Brazilian agriculture, increasing productivity and competitiveness.

QUALIFIED TRAINING

We contribute to the training of highly qualified Masters and PhDs through the INPE Remote Sensing Graduate Program (PGSER).
